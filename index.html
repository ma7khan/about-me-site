SPOKEN WORD

I am drawn to the art of spoken word because it has become my safe space to create and express myself freely with no judgements. It allows me to express my innermost thoughts in a poetic way, using imagery and metaphors without having to outright say how I feel if I donâ€™t want to. At first I kept my spoken words in the notes app on my phone, me being the only person to read them. But, I started performing my spoken words in 2018 at different youth open mic events. At first I was nervous to speak and share my innermost thoughts and feelings with the world, but soon I loved performing and couldn't get enough of it. It felt very powerful to stand up on stage in front of a bunch of strangers and speak my truth. I also started posting my pieces on Instagram and TikTok, mostly so that others know they are not alone in how they feel about certain situations. It has become this great community where I and other artists encourage one another to share our work and speak our truths.
